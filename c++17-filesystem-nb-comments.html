US 61 - 27.10.8.4.9 ¶ 8 [New] Leading dots in filename() should not begin an extension  

Comments

Leading dots in filename() should not be taken to begin an extension (e.g., .bashrc).

Proposed change

Follow Python’s path.splitext() in ignoring them.

Discussion

BGD suggests additional cases to be considered:

..abc
...abc
abc..def
abc...def
